Biography

I am a third-year PhD student at Department of Computing & Information Sciences, Rochester Institute of Technology, USA advised by Dr. Yunbo Zhang. Before that I received my master degree in the Department of Computer Science, Rochester Institute of Technology, USA, in August. 2021 (Advisor: Dr. Yu Kong) and B.E degree from School of Science, Qingdao University of Technology, China, in Jul. 2019.

My research interest broadly includes open set recognition, robot Learning, AR teleoperation systems for robots, and their applications related to computer vision tasks.
